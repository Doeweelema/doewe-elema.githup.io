Welcome to Rugby

For most people rugby will not be the first one to cross their minds while thinking about a sport. It is just not that famous and not many people know about it. 'Rugby for You' hopes to change that, we want you to know everything about this sport and no longer have all these question marks floating through your head when someone mentions the sport!
