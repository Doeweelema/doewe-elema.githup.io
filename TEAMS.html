Where is rugby located in the world?

When you hear the rugby-nation you might think of New Zealand, Australia or South Africa, the most wel known rugby countries. But in fact, rugby is played in more than 120 countries with over 8 million rugby players registered in competitions across Europe, Africa, both North and South America and the Asia-Pacific.
